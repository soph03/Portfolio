Overview

Protothon

An online UX Hackathon held by the University of Washington focused on prototyping and design thinking.

Agora is a company dedicated towards making Real-Time engement ubiquitous, allowing eveyone to interact with anyone, in any app.

Prompt

Design an enterprise real time engagment mobile or web application that showcases how Agoraâ€™s video conferencing/voice/streaming/messaging solutions can be used.

Problem

As everyone becomes heavily reliant on digital technology, building an appropriate digital enviroment is more than just satisfying the sense of sight. Networking has always been crucial for students as well as business stakeholders, however, it has become an greater problem for anyone looking to build meaningful conncetion.

Design Space

How might we emulate
spontanious interactions
virtually?
How might we create a digital environment that provides users the
space to roam
around and network?

Solution

Bump, a web application that simulates the spontaneous interaction of real life networking events amongst participants virtually, by leaveraging Agora's 3D Spatial Audio feature.

User Persona

Identifying a specific persona helped us identify product features that catered towards user needs.

User Journey

Initial touchpoints

  • Jade receives a bump notification through email
  • Arrives at the landing page of the specific event
  • Seamlessly connects with her Linkedln account

Entering the Event

Jade enters the event and can view the biography of attendees taken directly from the user's Linkedin profile.

Jade can move her avatar into the promixity of other conversations:

Floating, Speculator and Conversation

This enables the 3D Spatial audio* feature that provides Jade a simultaneous interation and real life environment

*3D Spatial Audio Prototype (drag neon circle in the corner)

Using data imported from Linkedln accounts, Bump can also provide Jade with personalized prompts to help guide her conversations.

This is driven by Natural Language Processing.

The Exit Experience

At the end of the event, Jade is given the names of attendees and speakers to ensure that she can reach out to anyone she missed.

She is also given a brief NPS survey on her experience with Bump.

View prototype

Conversation States

Floating

The Jade (user) cannot hear any of the conversations around her. The user can move around the surrounding area by holding and dragging her profile icon.

  • The undulating circle size appearing around Felica's profile icon indicates that she is speaking.
  • The line connecting from Felicia to other guest speaker indicates an on-going conversation between those users.

Spectating Conversation

Jade has now entered the first ring and can only hear Felicia talking but can't contribute to the conversation.

Entering Conversation

Jade is now in the inner ring and can now join the conversation amongst other guest speakers or users.

Reflection

One aspect I found challenging was recognizing what to focus on with the given time constraint. Unlike school settings where you have the time and resources to tackle every point in the design process, protothons force you to work smart by utilizing prior knowledge and applying skills from your of studies.
